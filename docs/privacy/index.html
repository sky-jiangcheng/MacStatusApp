MacStatus Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

MacStatus does not collect, store, share, or sell any personal data.

Data Collection

MacStatus does not collect analytics, tracking data, account information, contact information, location data, or identifiers.

Local Device Information

MacStatus uses public macOS APIs to display the current time and the list of applications currently running on your Mac. This information is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Permissions

MacStatus does not request access to your files, screen recording, keyboard input, network traffic, contacts, location, camera, microphone, or accessibility data.

Data Sharing

Because MacStatus does not collect personal data, it does not share personal data with third parties.
